"The camera is an instrument that teaches people how to see without a camera."

Here is an excerpt of my photography, these photos are my reminiscence of the past. From China to Europe, from Oceania to America, each photo is a projection of my study and life in the past years. They allow me to remember the past clearly, even if I can't go back to the same place.

It is difficult to find the same memories of the world you once saw, heard, and touched again. The people, objects or wind-blown ripples captured by the camera exist in a two-dimensional form on the screen, but they always contain that memory. When I opened these photos several years later, the wind blowing by the sea and the trains whistling through the city seemed to come back to me.
