Hi guys! This is Dallas Yang's personal photography page. I am so happy that you find this page, which is a secret place for me to share what I saw, what I felt, and what I thought. Here you can find the places where I traveled during the past years. Although my skills need to improve, but I hope I can share the beauty in the mundane life which I experienced with you.

These are all the equipments I used to create all the photos. I know they are not that ideal, but they are enough for me to capture the moving moments. They traveled with my friends and I for a long time and went through dozens of places. Though they are not the most advanced machines anymore, I still feel that they can be my great partners.
